Exploring a Decade of Water Quality Complaints in NYC: 2010 to 2019

Use this dashboard to explore different types of water quality complaints in New York City over the 10 year period from 2010 to 2019. Hover over a bar to see the borough breakdown for that complaint type and hover over the borough in the pie chart to see how many of each complaint type was made in that borough.

If you ever have a need to report a water quality issue in NYC, you can do so here. Other 311 related problems can be found and reported here.

Source: 311 Water Quality Service Requests from 2010 to 2019.
